# 📘 NLP Tech Class – Day 1

## 🧠 What is NLP?

**Natural Language Processing (NLP)** is a part of AI that helps computers understand human language.

👉 In simple words:  
It allows machines to **read, understand, and respond to text or speech like humans do**.

---

## 💡 Important Concept

👉 **Computers understand only numbers, not text.**

So in NLP:

"I love pizza" (text)  
⬇️ converted into  
[0.2, 0.8, 0.1] (numbers)

👉 These numbers are what the computer actually understands.

---

## 🔄 Simple Flow

**Text → Numbers → Computer understands → Output**

---

## 💡 Why this is needed?

- Words = human-friendly  
- Numbers = computer-friendly  

So we convert language into numbers to make machines understand it.

---

## 🧾 Example

Sentence:  
"I love pizza 🍕"

Converted into numbers → processed by model →  
Output: **Positive sentiment**

---

## 📌 Key Terms

- **Vectorization** → Converting text into numbers  
- **Text Embedding** → Representing meaning of words in number form  

---

## 🌍 Real-Life Examples

- Voice assistants (Siri, Alexa)  
- Chatbots (like ChatGPT)  
- Spam detection  
- Google Translate  
- Search engines  

---

## 📌 Key Takeaway

👉 NLP is the bridge between **human language** and **computers**.
